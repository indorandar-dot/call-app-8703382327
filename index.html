✨ Features

Sab kuch jo chahiye,
ek app mein

Messaging se leke video calls tak — CallingApp mein har cheez hai jo aapko chahiye.

💬
Instant Messaging
Real-time messages bhejo aur pao. Typing indicators, read receipts, aur emoji reactions ke saath.
📞
Voice Calls
Crystal clear voice calls karo apne contacts ke saath. Speaker aur mute controls built-in hain.
📹
Video Calls
Face-to-face video calling with HD quality. Chahe koi bhi phone ho, call clear aayegi.
👥
Group Chats
Apne friends aur family ke saath groups banao. Admin controls, invite links, sab kuch.
🤖
Powerful Bots
Apna khud ka bot banao BotFather se. Commands, auto-replies — sab customize karo.
🔒
Secure & Private
Aapka data safe hai. End-to-end encryption aur Firebase ki security ke saath.
🟢
Live Presence
Dekho kaun online hai real-time mein. Last seen aur typing status bhi dikhta hai.
📋
Call History
Apni sari calls ka record dekho. Voice, video, duration — sab organized rakha hai.
😊
Emoji Reactions
Kisi bhi message pe emoji se react karo. Conversations zyada fun aur expressive bante hain.
🚀 How It Works

3 steps mein shuru karo

Koi complicated setup nahi. 2 minute mein ready ho jao.

1
Sign Up karo
Apna email aur password daalo. Ek click mein account ban jayega — bilkul free.
2
Profile banao
Apna naam, emoji aur photo set karo. Dosto ko apna unique JG ID share karo taaki wo aapko add kar sakein.
3
Connect karo!
Dosto ko add karo, groups banao, baat karo aur calls shuru karo. Bas itna hi!
